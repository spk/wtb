wtb(1)
=======

NAME
----
wtb - print id or title of currently open bug

SYNOPSIS
--------
*wtb* [*-t* | *--title*] [*-v* | *--version*] [*-h* | *--help*]

DESCRIPTION
-----------
what the bug - print id or title of currently open bug on your X

OPTIONS
-------
*-t*, *--title*::
    Show bug title.
*-v*, *--version*::
    Show version.
*-h*, *--help*::
    Show help message.
